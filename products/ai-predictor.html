How the AI Predictor Works

01
Multi-Source Data Ingestion
84 input signals ingested in real time — LME/COMEX prices, DXY, central bank data, shipping indices, geopolitical risk scores, satellite imagery and supply chain telemetry.
02
Feature Engineering & Normalisation
Automated feature engineering pipeline extracts 240+ derived features — momentum indicators, cross-commodity correlations, seasonality patterns and regime detection signals.
03
Ensemble Model Inference
LSTM, Transformer and gradient boosting models run in parallel. A meta-learner ensemble combines outputs with dynamic weighting — optimised for the current market regime.
04
Scenario Generation & Confidence Scoring
Monte Carlo simulation generates 10,000 price paths. Bull, base and bear scenarios are extracted with calibrated confidence intervals and probability-weighted price targets.
05
Weekly Retraining & Drift Detection
Models retrain weekly on fresh data. Automated drift detection monitors prediction accuracy in real time — triggering emergency retraining if accuracy drops below threshold.
